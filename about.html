About.關於

在元智不知道要吃甚麼嗎? 常常無法抉擇有甚麼好吃的嗎?
我們提供各式餐點選擇，你可以藉由選單中的餐廳找尋早餐、午餐、晚餐， 並且提供地圖，你可以查詢餐廳的位置，容易前往該餐廳。 在Y‧Food‧Z，尋找元智周遭美食。

你可以這麼使用我們：
-從MENU中找到RESTAURANT，再從早餐、午餐、晚餐中選擇，依各項選擇會跳到你所選擇的頁面，你可在各個頁面看到簡介以及連結到各個網站。
-或是從首頁中的熱門推薦中，選擇我們為你推薦的餐廳，按下「MORE」即可連結到食記或是各個官網。

若有任何疑問，可將頁面往下拉到底，有我們的FACEBOOK粉專，我們很樂意為大家解答，或是可連結到我們的INSTAGRAM，可個別私訊我們喔~

Don’t know what to eat nearby YZU? Can’t choose what’s good for you?
We offer a variety of meal choices; you can click the menu for choosing breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Also, we provide map so that you can check out the location of the restaurant and go to there in an easy way.

You can use our website by:
-Using MENU to find the RESTAURANT and then choose from BREAKFAST, LUNCH, and DINNER. After you choose the options you will enter to the page, you can find a brief introduction and a link to their website.
-Or you can find out the recommend restaurant in our home page, choose the restaurant that we suggest you. Click MORE and then you can see detail from the website.

If there’s any question, you can see the bottom that we put our Facebook pages links and our personal Instagram on it. We are pleasure to the answer your question. Enjoying it!
